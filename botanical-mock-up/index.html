Aloe Vera

Aloe vera

Aloe vera is a succulent plant species of the genus Aloe. It is widely used in the cosmetic, pharmaceutical, and food industries for its various beneficial properties. The plant is also popular as a houseplant and for its ability to remove toxins from the air.

Snake Plant

Dracaena trifasciata

Snake Plant, also known as Sansevieria or Mother-in-Law's Tongue, is a popular indoor plant known for its hardiness and ability to tolerate low light conditions. Its tall, upright leaves are often variegated with yellow or white edges, making it an attractive addition to any space.

Spider Plant

Dracaena trifasciata

The Spider Plant, or Chlorophytum comosum, is a popular and easy-to-grow houseplant with long, arching leaves that are often variegated with white or cream stripes. The plant produces small white flowers and baby plantlets, which can be propagated easily to create more plants.
